"...and I'm totally inspired by nature and day to day living..."

I studied art at Mzilikazi Art and Craft Centre from 2003 to 2004. After that I became a resident artist at the National Gallery in Bulawayo from 2005-2009.
Over the years I have participated in a number of group exhibitions just to name a few "Young artist's Exhibition" Gallery Delta Harare 2007, "Thatha Nwele" National Gallery In Bulawayo in 2005.
I had my first solo exhibition in 2013, titles "Interface," at the National Gallery in Bulawayo.

Awards

  • Young Artist of Promise, 2006, VAAB Annual Exhibition.
  • Best artist under 30 " Annual Wild Geese Art Festival" Harare 2012.
